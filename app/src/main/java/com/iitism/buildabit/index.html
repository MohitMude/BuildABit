Our Story

FotoFreaks - The Photography Club Of IIT (ISM) is group of photography-enthusiasts here at IIT (ISM) and has two major objectives. Firstly, to act as a platform for bringing together the budding photographers of IIT (ISM) and providing them with opportunities for honing their skills. Secondly, it aims to be a collection of all sorts of IIT (ISM) memories, from photographs of lonely, empty roads to those of gigantic halls filled with loud, enthusiastic students; from photographs of the quiet, peaceful Heritage building during dawn to those of students sweating under the sun in rallies. We believe that photographs are a beautiful way of capturing moments and preserving memories, they take you back in time and make you rekindle the lost elation that you repeatedly feel during numerous experiences throughout your college life. We want to be more than just a popular page, we want to be a hub of memories, we want to be a time machine, and most importantly, we want to be a reason for the smile on your face. For associating with us or for any other queries regarding the events and activities of FotoFreaks , mail us on fotofreaks@iitism.ac.in
